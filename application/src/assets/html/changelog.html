Version 1.0

Released on: 01 September 2013

Initial release. Features:

  • Choose images from folders: Document, Camera, Photos & Downloads
  • Scale, rotate and move your images with your touch
  • Overlays: home screen, locked phone & active frames
  • Set edited image as device wallpaper or save it in folder Photos/Wappy
  • Use long press for the context menu
  • Use the device's cameras to take pictures from Wappy
  • Share our application and spread Wappy to the world
  • Keep track of changes made to Wappy on the Changelog screen
