Version 1.0

Released on: 2013-11-01

Initial release. Q10 only. Features:

  • Choose images from folders: Document, Camera, Photos & Downloads
  • Free multi touch image editor
  • Long Press Tutorial image to show action items
  • Frames: home screen, locked phone & active frames
  • Set image as device wallpaper
  • Save edited images on folder Photos/Wappy
  • Selected image is scaled and positioned exactly as the device does
  • Use the device's cameras to take pictures from the application
  • Share our application and spread the word to the world
  • Keep track of all changes we do on the app on the Change Log page.
  • Meet the people who created this app on the Creators Tab
